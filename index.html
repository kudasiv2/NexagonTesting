Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Nexagon

How do I start investing with Nexagon?

To start investing, connect your wallet using the "Connect Wallet" button, choose an investment plan that suits your goals, and deposit the required amount of USDT (BEP-20). Your investment will start generating returns immediately.

When can I withdraw my earnings?

You can withdraw your ROI earnings every 8 hours. Referral and leadership bonuses can be withdrawn at any time without restrictions.

How does the referral system work?

Our referral system has 15 levels with different commission percentages. You earn commissions from the withdrawals of users you refer and from users referred by your team, up to 15 levels deep. Each level has a minimum deposit requirement to unlock.

What are the leadership requirements?

Leadership levels are based on the number of active team members in your referral network. Bronze requires 200 active members, Silver requires 500, and Gold requires 1000 active team members with deposits.

Is my investment secure?

Nexagon is built on secure smart contract technology with transparency and security as our top priorities. All transactions are recorded on the blockchain and can be verified by anyone. However, as with any investment, there are inherent risks that should be considered.
