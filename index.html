Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform, investment plans, and referral program.

What is Nexagon and how does it work?

Nexagon is a decentralized investment platform built on blockchain technology. It allows users to invest in various plans and earn daily returns. The platform uses smart contracts to ensure transparency, security, and automated payouts.

How do I start investing with Nexagon?

To start investing, first connect your wallet using the "Connect Wallet" button. Then navigate to the "Invest" section, choose an investment plan that suits your goals, enter the amount you wish to invest, and confirm the transaction. Your investment will be active immediately.

What is the minimum and maximum investment?

The minimum and maximum investment amounts vary by plan. The Gold Plan requires a minimum of 10 USDT with a maximum of 100 USDT. The Platinum Plan requires 50 USDT minimum with a 100 USDT maximum. The Diamond Plan requires 100 USDT minimum with no maximum limit.

How does the referral program work?

Our referral program has 15 levels with different commission rates. When someone uses your referral link to join and invest, you earn a percentage of their investment and subsequent earnings. The commission rates range from 15% for Level 1 down to 1% for Levels 11-15, depending on your total investment amount.

When can I withdraw my earnings?

You can withdraw your ROI earnings every 8 hours once your investment is active. Referral bonuses can be withdrawn at any time as long as you have available balance. Simply go to the Dashboard section and click the "Withdraw ROI" or "Withdraw Referral" button.

Is my investment secure?

Yes, Nexagon is built on secure blockchain technology with audited smart contracts. All transactions are transparent and recorded on the blockchain. We use industry-standard security practices to protect user funds and data.
